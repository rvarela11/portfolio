About Me

For the past 4 years, I have built the customer support team for two start ups. One of my strategies was to set up a system that would track customer issues. This helped the company understand what the top issues were and find the best solutions. Unfortunately, the solutions would never get made because there were not enough engineers to build them. This was the deciding factor that made me realize that I wanted to become a software engineer. I want to be able to build solutions to provide a better customer experience.
